Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how this application uses data obtained through the TikTok API.

Information We Collect

The application may access the following data from TikTok accounts that explicitly authorize access:

  • Public profile information (username, profile picture)
  • Public video metadata (video IDs, titles, thumbnails)

How We Use the Information

The collected data is used solely to display information inside the application and to provide its core functionality.

Data Sharing

No data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

Data Storage

The application does not permanently store personal data.

User Control

Users may revoke the application's access to their TikTok account at any time through TikTok account settings.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
